Install Ranjan-AI on Chrome Desktop

1 Open Ranjan-AI in Chrome: Make sure you're using Google Chrome and visit the Ranjan-AI website.
2 Click the install icon: Look for the install icon in the address bar (it looks like a computer monitor with a down arrow).
3 Click "Install": A prompt will appear asking if you want to install Ranjan-AI. Click the "Install" button.
4 Access from your desktop: Once installed, Ranjan-AI will appear in your applications list and can be pinned to your taskbar or dock for easy access.

Install Ranjan-AI on Android

1 Open Ranjan-AI in Chrome: Visit the Ranjan-AI website using Chrome on your Android device.
2 Open Chrome menu: Tap the three dots in the top-right corner to open the Chrome menu.
3 Tap "Add to Home screen": From the dropdown menu, select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app".
4 Confirm installation: Tap "Add" or "Install" in the prompt that appears.
5 Access from your home screen: Ranjan-AI will now appear as an app icon on your home screen.

Install Ranjan-AI on iOS (iPhone/iPad)

1 Open Ranjan-AI in Safari: Visit the Ranjan-AI website using Safari on your iOS device. (Note: This won't work in Chrome on iOS)
2 Tap the Share button: Tap the Share icon (square with an arrow) at the bottom of the screen.
3 Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen": Scroll through the options in the share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4 Confirm installation: Tap "Add" in the top-right corner of the prompt.
5 Access from your home screen: Ranjan-AI will now appear as an app icon on your home screen.
